About Lolland Retreat Center

About Lolland Retreat Center

We are very happy to invite you to the Lolland course with Jamgon Kongtrul Rinpoche. It's a special visit since the previous incarnation of Jamgon Kongtrul gave the Kagyu Ngagdzö here in 1989.
The Lolland Center has a special history as the longest-running diamond way retreat center. It was founded in the 70's after Lama Ole and Hannah returned to Denmark from the East.
The center's special connection to Milarepa dates back to the time when the 16th Karmapa visited the place in 1977. When the 16th Karmapa came to the place, he had a vision of meeting Milarepa in the garden, who told him that "A Thousand Buddhas will fly from here”. During his visit, Karmapa gave the Milarepa empowerment and Black Crown Ceremony. Once again we can celebrate this close connection to Milarepa and attend the Milarepa empowerment given by Jamgon Kongtrul Rinpoche. Since then, the place has been blessed by the presence of many great masters, and we are happy to continue the tradition of summer courses since 1979. Come and enjoy this historical place.

About accommodation during the course

Camping in tents:

Please bring your tents, warm sleeping bags and clothes fitting for changing weather. The accommodation possibilities for this course is only camping.
Unfortunately, due to the size of the center, we have no possibility to offer indoor sleeping or accommodate special needs.

Autocampers:

You are welcome to camp with your autocamper. Unfortunately, we do not provide electricity or water for autocampers. Please register your autocamper by writing to lolland@buddha.dk.

Hotels and hostels:

Search at www.hostels.com or www.booking.com

Airbnb:

Transport:

Public transport:

There are several options to get to the course by public transport. The closest bus stop is “Søllested Kirke” (500 m) and the closest train station is “Søllested” (10 km).

From Copenhagen, you can take a train to Maribo. The train ride takes about 1 hour and 45 minutes. From Maribo, you can take a traint to Søllested Station where you can get a taxi or request a shiuttle to the course location. The taxi ride from Søllested Station to the course location takes about 15 minutes.

Check https://www.journeyplanner.dk/ for transport solutions and tickets.

Shuttle from Rødbyhavn ferry:

We will organise a shuttle from Rødbyhavn ferry to the course location. Please ask for the shuttle by writing to lolland@buddha.dk.


Children on site

At the center, we follow the principle of a "red line," a vision introduced by Hannah Nydahl. This includes a child-free zone designated for practitioners. The child-free area encompasses the gompa, the stupa yard, and the surroundings of the new retreat huts.

We kindly ask all parents to respect these boundaries and ensure their children avoid child-free areas. Children are allowed in the gompa tent, but please ensure they do not disturb the event or other participants.

All parents are fully responsible for the safety and well-being of their children. Please note that we do not provide organised childcare.
